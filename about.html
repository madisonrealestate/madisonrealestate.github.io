Who we are

About Us

Madison Real Estate Inc. is a full-service, family-owned real estate firm expertly representing investors in development, management, and brokerage.

With over 100 years of experience among its founders, Madison Real Estate Inc. has been able to perfect its strategies while providing its clients with the utmost in terms of transparency, partnership, and commitment. As we work with commercial, multi-family, and single-family real estate assets, we have harbored a broad perspective on various real estate ventures. This broad perspective lends itself to our clients, giving them a greater range of service and expertise.

As a family-owned firm, we are hyper-focused on delivering the highest value and best use for all of our real estate projects. We know how important it is to treat clients with respect, so we pride ourselves on fostering a culture of collaboration between ourselves and our clients. Our commitment to our clients is fundamental to us and surpasses the standard of other real estate businesses.

What we do

Our services

Brokerage

At Madison Real Estate Inc., we understand the inevitable ups-and-downs of the market. This market knowledge allows us to provide you with insights in buying a home or investing in a property. In addition, our brokers have cultivated great relationships within the Los Angeles area and will work hard to earn our clients the right sale at the right price.

Brokerage Services:

  • Marketing properties to achieve high visibility
  • Negotiating with potential buyers and sellers
  • Providing due diligence
  • 1031 exchange
  • Arranging inspections
  • Locating potential properties for buyers
  • Working with commercial, multi-family residences, and single-family homes

Property Management

We offer full property management services for commercial and multi-family properties. As property managers, our team has a proactive and hands-on approach in dealing with tenants and vendors. We believe in great customer service to maintain outstanding relationships among tenants, owners, and managers.

Property management services:

  • Attracting tenants through various marketing strategies
  • Obtaining contracts with tenants and negotiating leases
  • Maintaining properties (i.e. landscaping and repairs)
  • Hiring administrators and staff
  • Analyzing financial objectives

Construction

We have vast experience in the development of multi-family residences, commercial properties, and single-family homes. With our experience, we are ready to skillfully oversee your project from inception to completion. Moreover, our team will provide you with a budget and timeline for the project you have at hand.

Construction services:

  • Dealing with all types of real estate assets (i.e. commercial, residential, and remodeling)
  • Arranging all permits and inspections
  • Providing skilled labor
  • Hiring and directing specialized contractors
  • Creating a timeline and scheduling work to be performed under the timeline
  • Supervising the construction site
